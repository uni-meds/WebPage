About Us

Start Up aiming to make your healthcare a better experience.

Founded in 2018, We aim to provide people with low cost generic medicines. Generic Medicines are copies of brand-name drugs that have exactly same dosage, effects, risks, strength etc as original drug. These are fairly low cost and have identical pharmacological effects. We aim to form the biggest network in India for providing Generic Medicines along with other medical equipments at a low cost.
